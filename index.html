Definitions

  1. Internal Hyperlink: A hyperlink that points to a page or a section
  2. External Hyperlink: An external hyperlink, also known as an outbound link, is a hyperlink that points to a different domain or website than the one the hyperlink exists on. Clicking on an external hyperlink takes you to a different website or webpage.
  3. Graphical Hyperlink:A graphical hyperlink, also known as an image link, is a hyperlink that is represented by an image or graphic rather than text. When the image is clicked, the user is taken to the linked webpage.
  4. Bookmark: A bookmark, also known as a favorite, is a saved link to a specific webpage that allows users to quickly return to that page. Bookmarks are saved in a browser's bookmark or favorites menu, and can also be organized into folders for easy access.
